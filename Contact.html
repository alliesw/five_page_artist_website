Joey Scott Art

Located in Boulder Colorado, Joey Scott is an emerging mixed media artist who utilizes various digital technologies to elevate her freehand and watercolor artwork. Her art style reflects both abstraction and expressionalism, as she seeks to reduce artistic objects and human subjects to their most basic form - in effort to express their true essence and provide a unique lens into her interpretation of the object.
